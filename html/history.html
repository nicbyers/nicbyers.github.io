History

Take a trip down memory lane and explore the past from the 1940s through the late 90s. And hey, if you have any photos to share, we'd love to see them! Don't be shy, drop us a line via the Contact page.

1940s

Let's start with the 1940s. These pictures come straight from a scrapbook in the Chapter Archies room and most of them are from 1947. You'll see most of them are from 1947. You'll see snapshots of the good ol' days, including some epic Derby Days games on Fetzer field. And let us tell you, Fetzer field looked quiet different back then! If you're in the area, stop by the house and check out these photos in person. And if you happen to know any Alpha Tau alumni from the 1940s, share these pictures with them or upload them to the website for all to see. Let's keep those memories alive!

1980s

Get ready to take a groove trip back in time to the 1980s! We've got some fantastic photos from this era that are sure to bring back some memories. These pictures capture a unique moment in history, with the hairstyles, fashion, and music all reflecting the spirit of the times. From wild parties to serious study sessions, these photos give us a glimpse into what life was like for Alpha Tau brothers during this exciting decade. So, if you're an Alpha alum from the 80s, or just a fan of retro coolness, be sure to check out these photos andrelieve the glory days.

1990s

Let's fast forward to the 1990s, a decade that brought us flannel shirts, grunge music, and some unforgettable Alpha Tau moments. Our collection of photos from the 90s showcases the energy and enthusiasm of Alpha Tau brother during this era. You'll see images of epic parties, group outings, and unforgettable bonding experiences that helped shape the brotherhood we know today. Whether you were an Alpha Tau during the 90s or just a fan of the decade, these photos are a must-see. So, put on your Doc Martens and crank up the Nirvana, and take a journey back in time to the Alpha Tau house of the 90s.
