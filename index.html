Welcome Brothers

The mission of the Alpha Tau chapter of Sigma Chi Fraternity at UNC Chapel Hill is to develop and empower our members to be principled leaders who live by the values of friendship, justice, and learning. Through our commitment to academic excellence, philanthropy, and community service, we strive to make a positive impact on the lives of those around us.

We aim to cultivate a culture of inclusivity, respect, and integrity within our brotherhood and beyond, and to uphold the high standards set forth by our national organization. As Sigma Chis, we are dedicated to fostering lifelong relationships and creating a legacy of excellence that extends far beyond our time on campus.
